◇ CONTROLS ◇

↑ ↓ / W S : Move paddle

Mouse / Touch : Drag vertically

1 / 2 / 3 : Use item

◇ HOW TO WIN ◇

First to 7 points wins the match.

Score by getting the ball past the foe's paddle.

◇ PADDLE ◇

Zigzag shape — middle bulge is a sweet spot: center hits bounce back faster with sharper angles.

◇ SELF ITEMS ◇

  • ⚡ LONG — Paddle ×1.7 for 8s
  • 🧲 MAGNET — Ball pulled to your paddle 5s
  • 🔥 POWER — Your NEXT hit returns at high speed (ball turns orange)
  • 🛡️ GHOST — Ball turns green; next time it reaches foe's paddle, it phases through
  • 🏹 CURVE — Paddle becomes concave 7s; edge hits return at dramatic angles
  • 🎲 CHAOS — Paddle turns jagged 6s; bounces are unpredictable (for you too!)

◇ SABOTAGE ◇

  • 🐢 SLOW — Foe paddle slowed 5s
  • 🌀 FLIP — Reverse foe controls 5s
  • 📏 SHRINK — Foe paddle ×0.5 for 6s
  • 👻 FOG — Blur foe's view 5s

◇ ITEM SPAWN ◇

Items appear on court every ~3s (up to 2 at once). Hit them with the ball to collect.
